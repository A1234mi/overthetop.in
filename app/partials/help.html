Overview

Over The Top lets you search for stakeholder's comments in the dataset made public by TRAI.

Introduction

In March 2015, India's telecom regulator TRAI released a consultation paper on regulating Over The Top (OTT) services. If not for the fine folks at #SaveTheInternet, this consultation process may have gone unnoticed without much debate. The campaign generated over 1 million responses from stakeholders and created a unique dataset like never seen before.

Searching

Comments can be searched by name, email address, and comments. All fields are optional and default to search all if left empty. Search results are limited to first 100 results, because performance. If you know how to get pagination working, ping back.

How do you handle email addresses?

A hash of stakeholder's email addresses is stored and used for lookup purposes.
