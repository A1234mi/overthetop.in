Explore stakeholder's comments on regulation of OTT services

In March 2015, India's telecom regulator TRAI released a consultation paper on regulating Over The Top (OTT) services. If not for the fine folks at #SaveTheInternet, this consultation process may have gone unnoticed without much debate. The campaign generated over 1 million responses from stakeholders and created a unique dataset like never seen before. Read more →

How to search?

Use the search form at the top and enter a name, or an email address, or comment text and hit Search. All fields are optional.
